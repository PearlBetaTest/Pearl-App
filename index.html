Track Your Request

How It Works

1

User Submits Request

Fill out our simple form with details about what you need. Include specifications, budget, and timeline.

2

Request Reviewed

Our team reviews your request and assigns it to a specialist team member with expertise in sourcing your specific item.

3

Sourcing & Options Proposed

Your assigned team member will research local suppliers and reach out with purchasing options, prices, and availability details.

4

Option Selection

Review the presented options and select your preferred choice based on price, quality, and delivery timeline.

5

Payment

Pay your team member for the item cost and service fee. Multiple payment methods accepted.

6

Delivery Coordination

You and your team member will agree on the best delivery method - pickup, local delivery, or shipping. Additional delivery fees may apply.

7

Item Delivery

Receive your item according to the agreed delivery method and timeline. Request completed!

Note: This process may differ when providing services as compared to goods. Service requests may involve different evaluation and delivery methods.

Frequently Asked Questions

Currently serving the Nairobi area. We're working to expand to other major cities soon.

Trusted team members with hospitality and concierge training handle all sourcing requests. Our team has extensive experience in local markets and supplier networks.

Our service fee is 500 KES per request. This covers the research, sourcing, and coordination. You'll pay separately for the actual item cost and any delivery fees.

Most requests receive initial options within two to three days unless otherwise specified. Complex or rare items may take longer. We'll keep you updated throughout the process.

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and cash payments. Payment details will be provided by your assigned team member once options are found.

If you're not satisfied with the initial options, we'll continue searching at no additional service fee. You only pay when you find something you want to purchase.

We coordinate delivery options including pickup and local delivery. Delivery arrangements and costs are discussed with your team member based on the item and location.

We can source most legal items including electronics, household goods, automotive parts, books, and more. We cannot assist with restricted, illegal, or hazardous items.
